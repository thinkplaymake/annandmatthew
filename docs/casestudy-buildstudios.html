When gofrendly wanted to come to the UK, they wanted to make sure their story would translate - Ann + Matthew rapidly refined the brand story, and rebooted a new, stronger tone for their product and content marketing.

THE STARTING POINT

gofrendly is a Stockholm-based start-up that makes it easier for women to make more friends.

Founded by real-life best friends Claudia Gård and Ulrika Lilja, gofrendly had already converted 80% of Stockholm’s women into monthly active users.

But their tone of voice wasn’t going to work in the UK. With 8 weeks to go until launch, they needed help, and they needed it quickly.

THE CHALLENGE

We asked gofrendly to dig through their archives. Discover what bones already existed. Allow us to locate their strengths and their gaps.

They located internal documents — decks, guides, brand frameworks. Everything you’d expect a busy start-up to own, but every document told a slightly different story. The brand wasn’t aligned. The founders couldn’t express who gofrendly really is, and it was frustrating them.

Building a tone of voice on top of an ill-defined brand wouldn’t address the problem. Despite the tight timeline, we believed we could deliver a refined brand, tone of voice and copy for web, app and email. All in 8 weeks.

OUR APPROACH

The founders were wrapped up in too many versions of their story - none of which they felt were right. So we took a step back, and asked Claudia and Ulrika tell their own story in their own words.

We located the true problem they’re solving. We helped them rediscover their purpose, and turned that into a clear positioning for their business.

The result?

Ulrika and Claudia, beaming. Two founders saying “Yes, that’s us”.
